Automated Decision Making

Automated decisions help out with every day activities without involving any human interaction.This ranges from having a robotic vacuum cleaner to having UAVs in a much larger scale.


Benefits

It helps remove human depency which is great for workplace environments as it means empolyees can focus on their own jobs without having to keep completing iterative jobs. It also makes decisions fast and error free, simultaneously ensuring consistency.


Risks

Challenges may also occur from time to time as sometimes their may be needs for human interventions on certain decisions which could cause set backs and delays until the human input is entered.Furthermore, there is always security risks with disruptions in code and woudl need to be attended to which can stop and also cause delays in the decision making.
